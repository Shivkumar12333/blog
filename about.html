📖 About This Blog

Welcome to MyBlog, a sleek and accessible platform where creativity meets simplicity. Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Firebase Firestore, this blog empowers anyone to share their voice with the world effortlessly.

🎯 Our Purpose

MyBlog exists to create a space where thoughts flow freely. Whether you're a writer, thinker, or explorer, this blog helps you focus on expressing ideas without distraction—simple, minimalist, powerful.

✨ Features

  • 📝 Clean and easy blog editor
  • 📂 Category-based organization and pagination
  • 🖼️ Image, video, and YouTube media support
  • ⚡ Real-time Firebase Firestore backend
  • 💬 Likes and comments with authentication
  • 🎨 Fully responsive, modern dark theme
